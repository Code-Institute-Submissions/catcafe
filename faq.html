Frequently Asked Questions

 

Can I bring my own pet?

We do not allow other animals apart from guide dogs into the cafe.

 

Are children allowed?

Unfortunately, we do not allow children younger than 10 at the cafe. Older children must be accompanied by an adult.

 

Do you accept large parties?

Yes, we do but please call in advance so we can change seating arrangements for you accordingly.

 

Are your foods labelled with allergy information?

Yes and you can tell staff if you have any dietary requirements.

 

Can I book the cafe for a private event?

Yes but please book at least 2 weeks in advance.

 

Do you have disabled access?


Yes, we do.

 

Have any other questions? Send us a message on our Contact Page.
