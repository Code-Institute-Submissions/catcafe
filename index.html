Welcome to the Cat Cafe!

 

Who We Are

 

We are the Cat Cafe! Local to Manchester, we allow customers to have a coffee while enjoying the company of our cats.

 

What We Do

 

We take in shelter cats, allowing customers to get to know them and get rehomed!

 

Reasons to visit us

  • Cats, cats and more cats!
  • We grind our own fresh beans, right in-store!
  • We help to get shelter cats new homes!
